Project Overview

DV Hive operates in a highly specialized segment of the automotive industry: automated post-accident appraisal services. Their audience consists of car owners who have recently been in an accident and need quick, reliable evaluations. Because this group is small, time-sensitive, and unpredictable, paid acquisition required precision. Synaps partnered with DV Hive to build and optimize Google Ads campaigns capable of capturing these users at the right moment, in the right states, and with a limited budget. The collaboration has grown into a long-term effort focused on refining conversion quality and maximizing impact.

Process

  1. Audience mapping to understand timing, intent, and user behavior

  2. Structuring campaigns at the state level for controlled budget and performance tracking

  3. Setting up search and call ads optimized for immediate action

  4. Weekly optimization cycles focused on keyword refinement, bidding, and ad copy

  5. Running A/B tests on messaging and targeting

  6. Integrating analytics to ensure accurate conversion tracking

  7. Providing DV Hive with direct access to reports, plus guidance on reading performance data

  8. Maintaining ongoing coordination with DV Hives team to align messaging and strategy
